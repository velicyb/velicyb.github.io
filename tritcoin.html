Tritcoin

Tritcoin is made up of prompts and steps rather than blocks, forming what we will call a stairchain.
A step is a trinary string consisting of 0s, 1s, and ?s.
A prompt consists of a step in which characters may be replaced with _s and _s may be inserted at the end of the stairchain.
Preference for which step follows the previous one is decided using a "distance" metric akin to Kademalia routing.
Tritcoin is designed to operate as a DAG of numerous stairchains, where a single stairchain is able to function as a keyless personal ledger.
